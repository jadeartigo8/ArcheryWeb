Our Story

Tacoma's Home for Archery Education Since 2005

Founded by Giap Nguyen, Straight to the Point Archery is a non-profit archery education center combining detailed technical video analysis with generations of archery expertise from certified coaches. We welcome everyone — from first-time archers to elite competitors.

We are dedicated to teaching valuable life skills to the youth in our community through the wonderful discipline of archery.

— Giap Nguyen, Founder & Owner
