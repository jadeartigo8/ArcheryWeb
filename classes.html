Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first session.

Do I need my own equipment?

No — all bows, arrows, arm guards, and finger tabs are included in every class and lesson fee. You will need to bring your own safety kit. Contact us for details on what the safety kit includes and where to purchase one.

What is the safety kit and where do I get one?

The safety kit includes personal protective gear required for all participants. Please reach out before your first class and we'll point you to where to get one locally or online.

Do I need to take A101 before A102?

Yes — A101 is the prerequisite for A102. The exception is if you receive a direct recommendation from one of our private lesson instructors, who can assess your existing skill level. A102 and A103 both require verified prior experience.

What is the minimum age for classes?

A101 starts at age 8 (age 7 with a pre-enrollment evaluation). A102 and A103 are open from age 7. Private lessons are available for all ages — please contact us to discuss the right fit for younger students.

Can someone with a disability participate?

Absolutely. Our facility is fully accessible and we have specific adaptive equipment available. Please contact us in advance so we can prepare the right setup for your first visit.

What should I wear?

Comfortable, close-toed shoes and form-fitting clothing on your bow arm — loose sleeves can catch the bowstring. Avoid dangling jewelry. That's it!

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancel at least 24 hours before your session for a full credit toward a future booking. Within 24 hours, sessions are non-refundable, but we'll do our best to reschedule if there's an emergency — just call us directly.
