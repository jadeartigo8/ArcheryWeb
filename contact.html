Planning Your Visit

Getting Here & What to Expect

Everything you need to know before your first visit to Archery Club.

Free Parking

On-site parking directly adjacent to the building. Accessible spaces near the main entrance. Street parking also available on Nock Point Ln.

Accessibility

Full wheelchair access throughout. Accessible restrooms and range lanes. Adaptive equipment available — please let us know in advance so we can prepare.

What to Wear

Comfortable, close-toed shoes and form-fitting clothing on your bow arm. Avoid dangling jewelry. We provide all bows and arrows.

Arrive Early

For your first session, arrive 10 minutes early to complete a waiver and get equipment fitted. Returning students — 5 minutes is plenty.

Safety Kit Required

All classes require a personal safety kit. Contact us before your first class and we'll let you know exactly what you need and where to get it.

Waiting Area

Parents and guests are welcome in our comfortable waiting area while students shoot. We have seating and a viewing area overlooking the range.
