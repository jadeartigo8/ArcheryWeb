Traditional & Outdoor Archery

Derek Jenkins

A Washington native and experienced outdoorsman, Derek brings a rare combination of wilderness expertise and hands-on craftsmanship to archery instruction — including handcrafting his own longbows and primitive arrows.

USA Archery Certified Coach · Safety Requirements Qualified · USAA Member

Derek's mastery of bushcraft and wilderness survival defines his approach to traditional archery. With many years of hunting, fishing, skiing, canoeing, and minimalist camping under his belt, he brings deep practical knowledge of the outdoors to every lesson. He handcrafts his own longbows, flint-knapped primitive arrows, and custom leather products — giving his students a truly authentic connection to the roots of the sport.

Whether you're brand new to a bow or want to explore the tradition behind it, Derek's instruction grounds students in the fundamentals while inspiring a deeper appreciation for archery as a craft.

Safety & Youth Programs

Amy Smith

Amy truly has a passion for archery. Coming from a long line of hunters, fishermen, and trappers in Canada, she combines a deep respect for the sport with 18+ years of YMCA fitness and personal training experience.

USA Archery Certified Coach · YMCA Group Exercise Instructor · Certified Personal Trainer

Safety is the cornerstone of Amy's teaching philosophy. Her enthusiasm for instruction is evident from the very first class, and students consistently appreciate her encouraging, high-energy approach. Her 18+ years as a YMCA group exercise and personal training instructor have sharpened her ability to read individual students, adapt coaching styles, and make every session productive and enjoyable.

Amy is an especially strong fit for youth learners and anyone new to the sport, bringing the patience and positivity that beginning archers need to build confidence at the range.

Competitive & NTS Coaching

Nathan Lee

USA Archery Level 3 NTS Coach teaching since 2009, Nathan's background as a martial arts instructor and studio manager gives him a unique edge in coaching archers of all ages and disciplines.

USA Archery Level 3 NTS Coach · Martial Arts Instructor · Studio Management Experience

Nathan's coaching philosophy centers on discipline, precision, and continuous improvement. His experience in martial arts has given him a deep understanding of the mental side of performance sports — skills that translate directly into competitive archery coaching. He excels at breaking down complex technique into clear, actionable steps that archers at any level can apply immediately.

Whether you're looking to compete nationally or simply improve your personal best, Nathan's systematic approach and encyclopedic knowledge of the NTS method will help you get there.
