About Archery Club

A non-profit archery education center founded in 2005 — where beginners become champions and community is our greatest achievement.

2005 Founded
Our Beginning

Founded on Passion, Built on Community

Archery Club is a non-profit archery education center founded in 2005 by Giap Nguyen in Tacoma, Washington. What began as a vision for accessible, expert archery education has grown into one of the Pacific Northwest's most respected facilities — serving everyone from first-time archers to professional competitors.

Our state-of-the-art facility combines detailed technical video analysis with generations of archery experience from certified coaches and instructors. We believe that archery teaches far more than accuracy — it builds focus, discipline, patience, and confidence that extend far beyond the range.

As a non-profit, every decision we make is driven by our community, not by profit. We exist to make great archery education accessible to everyone in Tacoma and beyond.

What Drives Us

Mission & Values

We teach in a positive learning environment with instructors who hold extensive knowledge of advanced archery equipment and all forms of archery — from the traditional longbow to the modern compound bow. Our mission is simple: help every archer reach their potential.

Safety First

Every session starts with range safety. Our USA Archery-certified instructors meet rigorous safety qualifications and instill safe habits that last a lifetime.

Community

As a non-profit, we are genuinely invested in the Tacoma community. We partner with schools, the YMCA, Boy and Girl Scouts, and other youth organizations to bring archery to more people.

Inclusion

We welcome archers of all ages, abilities, backgrounds, and experience levels. From beginner to elite, and from recreational to competitive — everyone belongs here.

Education

Our instructors combine detailed technical video analysis with deep personal experience to give every student the clearest, most effective path to improvement.

Excellence

We maintain strong connections with USA Archery, WSAA, NFAA, and other associations — ensuring our coaching standards remain at the highest level.

Youth Development

We are dedicated to teaching valuable life skills — focus, patience, discipline, and resilience — to young people in our community through the sport of archery.

Our Range

The Facility & Services

A state-of-the-art indoor and outdoor training center with everything you need under one roof.

14 Ten-Yard Lanes

Climate-controlled indoor range with 14 ten-yard lanes — perfect for beginners and form work.

14 Twenty-Yard Lanes

14 additional indoor twenty-yard lanes for intermediate and advanced shooting practice.

26 Extra Indoor Lanes

Access to 26 additional twenty-yard lanes for high-volume training days and events.

Outdoor Shooting Field

Full outdoor field access for field archery, long-distance practice, and bowhunting training.

2 Private Coaching Rooms

Dedicated private coaching rooms for 1-on-1 instruction, video analysis, and equipment fitting.

Technical Video Analysis

State-of-the-art video analysis tools used by coaches to provide detailed form feedback.

Fully Accessible

Wheelchair-accessible lanes, parking, restrooms, and adaptive equipment available on request.

Equipment Provided

All bows and arrows provided for classes — from beginner recurves to advanced compounds.

Our Commitment

Nondiscriminatory Policy

Archery Club admits students of any race, gender presentation, sexuality, religion, national origin, or ethnic origin to all rights, privileges, programs, and activities available at our center. We do not discriminate in administering our educational policies, admission policies, or other center-administered programs. All students are welcome and valuable members of our community.
